# Company Vacation and Leave Policy

## Effective Date: January 1, 2024

### 1. Vacation Time

All full-time employees are entitled to paid vacation time as follows:

- **0-2 years of service**: 15 days per year
- **3-5 years of service**: 20 days per year
- **6+ years of service**: 25 days per year

Vacation days accrue monthly and can be used after completing 90 days of employment.

### 2. Sick Leave

Employees receive 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave can be used for:
- Personal illness or injury
- Medical appointments
- Care for immediate family members who are ill

Unused sick leave does not roll over to the next year.

### 3. Personal Days

All employees receive 5 personal days per year for personal matters that cannot be scheduled outside of work hours.

### 4. Parental Leave

- **Maternity Leave**: 16 weeks of paid leave
- **Paternity Leave**: 8 weeks of paid leave
- **Adoption Leave**: 12 weeks of paid leave

### 5. Bereavement Leave

Employees are granted 5 days of paid bereavement leave in the event of a death in the immediate family.

### 6. Public Holidays

The company observes the following public holidays:
- New Year's Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving Day
- Christmas Day

### 7. Request Process

To request time off:
1. Submit a request through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance
2. Obtain manager approval
3. Ensure adequate coverage for your responsibilities
4. Receive confirmation from HR

### 8. Carryover Policy

Unused vacation days can be carried over to the next year, up to a maximum of 10 days. Days exceeding this limit will be forfeited.

### 9. Payout on Termination

Upon termination of employment, employees will be paid for all accrued but unused vacation days at their current rate of pay.
